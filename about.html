Our story

Dating apps measure
everything except the
one thing that matters.

Attune was built on a simple conviction: that how you feel in response to the world around you is the deepest signal of who you are. And that the right person for you responds to that same world in a way that resonates with yours.

The problem

A decade of swiping.
Still the same result.

Modern dating apps were built on the assumption that attraction is visual and compatibility is self-reported. You upload your best photos. You write a carefully crafted bio. You swipe on faces. And then you're surprised when the conversation goes nowhere.

The problem isn't that people are bad at dating. It's that the tools are measuring the wrong thing. A photograph tells you what someone looks like. A questionnaire tells you what someone thinks they want. Neither tells you how someone feels — and feeling is where real connection begins.

We started Attune to fix that. Not with another algorithm that optimises for engagement. With science that actually understands human emotion.

What we believe

Principles we don't
negotiate on.

🧬
Authenticity over performance

Your involuntary emotional responses are the most honest thing about you. We build technology to read those signals — not to let you game them. The system rewards being real.

🔒
Privacy by architecture

We made a fundamental design decision: raw emotional data never leaves your device. What we store is a mathematical pattern, not a biometric record. Your face is yours.

🧠
Science, not pseudoscience

EchoDepth is built on Paul Ekman's FACS system — decades of peer-reviewed psychology. We don't make claims we can't evidence. Emotion AI is hard. We do it properly.

🌍

Our models were trained across 6 countries and 14 cultures. Emotional expression varies across backgrounds. We built Attune to work for everyone — not just one demographic.

🚫

We will never sell your data to advertisers. We will never accept payment to show you a particular profile. Our incentives are aligned with yours: we succeed when you find someone real.

The technology

Emotion AI built by the people who wrote the science.

Attune is powered by EchoDepth — the emotional intelligence engine developed by Cavefish AI, a Cardiff-based company specialising in real-time facial Action Unit analysis.

EchoDepth analyses the 42 muscle movements that make up human facial expression — the FACS system originally developed by psychologist Paul Ekman — and maps them to emotional states in real time. No wearables, no contact sensors, no hardware beyond your phone's front camera.

Your emotional profile is processed entirely on your device. What we store is an anonymised emotional vector: a mathematical fingerprint of how you respond. It cannot be reverse-engineered to reconstruct your face or identify you.
