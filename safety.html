Safety Centre

Last updated: 1 March 2026

Safety is foundational to Attune — not an afterthought. We've built anti-fraud and authenticity verification into the core technology. But technology alone isn't enough.

In immediate danger?

Call 999 (UK) or your local emergency services. Non-emergency police: 101.

Urgent Attune safety report: safety@attunechemistry.com

How Attune protects you

🎭

Zero fake profiles

Every profile requires a live emotion-capture session. Bots and catfish cannot fake involuntary micro-expressions in real time.

🔒

On-device processing

Your raw emotional data and facial video never leave your device. We store only an anonymised mathematical vector.

🚨

In-app reporting

Report any user or conversation in one tap. All reports are reviewed by our safety team within 24 hours.

🚫

Block and unmatch

Block or unmatch anyone instantly. Blocked users cannot see your profile or contact you.

👁

Human moderation

Our safety team reviews all reports. Accounts violating community standards are permanently banned.

🔞

Age verification

Attune is strictly 18+. Age is verified at registration. Suspected underage accounts are immediately suspended.

Staying safe on the platform

Keep personal details private

Don't share your home address, workplace, phone number, or financial information with someone you've just matched with. Take time to build trust.

Recognise scam patterns

Be cautious of anyone who asks for money or gift cards for any reason, claims to be abroad and can't meet, wants to move off Attune quickly, or professes strong feelings very early.

Before meeting in person

  • Always meet in a public place. Never at a private location for a first meeting.
  • Arrange your own transport. Tell a friend where you're going and when to expect you back.
  • Keep your phone charged. Have an exit plan — you don't owe anyone an explanation.
  • Have a video call first to confirm they are who they say they are.

If something has happened to you

Contact the police if a crime has occurred (999 emergency, 101 non-emergency). Reach out to Rape Crisis (0808 500 2222) or Victim Support (0808 168 9111). For non-consensual image abuse: StopNCII.

Contact our safety team at safety@attunechemistry.com — we will preserve relevant account data and cooperate fully with law enforcement.
