Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

This policy explains how Attune uses cookies and similar technologies. We keep this as minimal as possible — we have no interest in tracking you beyond what's needed to make Attune work.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. Some are essential to the site functioning; others are optional.

What we use

CookieTypePurposeDuration
attune_sessionEssentialMaintains your login session.Session
attune_csrfEssentialSecurity token against cross-site request forgery.Session
attune_consentEssentialStores your cookie preference.1 year
_plausibleAnalyticsPrivacy-first analytics via Plausible. No personal data. No cross-site tracking.Session

What we don't use

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, Facebook or Google ad tags, or any technology designed to build a profile of your browsing behaviour across other websites. There are no ads on Attune.

Essential vs optional

Essential cookies

Necessary for Attune to function. You cannot opt out while using the platform.

Analytics (optional)

We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first tool that does not collect personal data or track you across sites. Enable "Do Not Track" in your browser to opt out; we honour it.

Managing cookies

Control cookies through your browser: Chrome · Firefox · Safari · Edge.
