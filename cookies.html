Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

This policy explains how Attune (operated by Cavefish AI Ltd) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and platform. We keep this as minimal as possible — we have no interest in tracking you beyond what's needed to make Attune work.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember preferences, maintain sessions, and understand how the site is being used. Some cookies are essential to the site functioning; others are optional.

What we use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
attune_session Essential Maintains your login session so you don't have to sign in on every page. Session
attune_csrf Essential Security token that protects against cross-site request forgery attacks. Session
attune_consent Essential Stores your cookie preference so we don't ask you every visit. 1 year
_plausible Analytics Privacy-friendly analytics via Plausible. No personal data is collected. No cross-site tracking. Fully GDPR-compliant. Session

What we don't use

We do not use advertising cookies, third-party tracking pixels, Facebook or Google ad tags, or any technology designed to build a profile of your browsing behaviour across other websites. There are no ads on Attune.

Essential vs optional cookies

Essential cookies

These are necessary for Attune to function. You cannot opt out of essential cookies while using the platform. They do not collect personal information beyond what is required for security and session management.

Analytics cookies (optional)

We use Plausible Analytics — a privacy-first analytics tool that does not use cookies in the traditional sense and does not collect any personal data or track you across sites. It simply tells us aggregate numbers like how many people visited a page. You can opt out of even this by enabling "Do Not Track" in your browser settings, which we honour.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent Attune from functioning correctly. Here are links to cookie management guides for common browsers:

  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Changes to this policy

If we add new cookies or change how we use existing ones, we'll update this page and, where appropriate, notify you via the cookie consent banner on your next visit.
