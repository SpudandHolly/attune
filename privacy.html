Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

Attune is operated by Cavefish AI Ltd, a company registered in England and Wales. We take your privacy seriously — especially because we handle something as personal as your emotional data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over it.

1. Who we are

Cavefish AI Ltd ("Attune", "we", "us") is the data controller for personal data collected through the Attune platform. Our registered address is Cardiff, Wales, United Kingdom. You can contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@attune.love.

2. What data we collect

Account data

When you register, we collect your first name, surname, email address, date of birth, and location (city level). We use this to create and manage your account.

Emotional profile data

Our core technology — EchoDepth, developed by Cavefish AI — analyses your facial expressions during a short video session. This analysis happens entirely on your device. We never transmit or store raw video footage or images of your face. What we retain is an anonymised emotional vector: a mathematical representation of your emotional response patterns. This vector contains no biometric identifiers and cannot be reverse-engineered to reconstruct your image.

Usage data

We collect standard usage data including app interactions, session times, and feature engagement. This helps us improve the product and diagnose issues.

Communications

If you contact us or sign up to the waitlist, we store your name, email, and the content of your message.

3. Legal basis for processing

  • Contract performance — to provide the Attune matching service you've signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — to improve the platform, prevent fraud, and ensure safety.
  • Consent — for marketing communications. You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to retain data by law.

For emotional profile data, which may constitute special category data under UK GDPR, we rely on your explicit consent given at the point of onboarding.

4. How we use your data

  • To generate your emotional profile and find compatible matches.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Attune platform.
  • To communicate with you about your account, matches, and product updates.
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and safety violations.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

5. Who we share data with

We do not sell your personal data to any third party. We share data only with:

  • Cavefish AI Ltd — who provide the EchoDepth processing technology under a data processing agreement.
  • Infrastructure providers — cloud hosting and database services operating under GDPR-compliant contracts.
  • Payment processors — if applicable, operating under PCI-DSS compliance.
  • Law enforcement — only when legally required to do so.

6. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Emotional vectors are retained for the duration of your membership and deleted within 30 days of account deletion. Waitlist data is retained until the public launch and then purged or converted to account records with your permission.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (right to erasure).
  • Restrict or object to processing.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is consent-based.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@attune.love. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate the platform and optional analytics cookies to understand usage. See our Cookie Policy for full details.

9. International transfers

We store and process data within the UK and EEA. Any transfers outside these regions are conducted under UK GDPR-approved safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. Children

Attune is not available to anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately at safety@attune.love.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email or in-app notification. Continued use of Attune after changes constitutes acceptance.
